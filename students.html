Great Southern Grammar - Student Portal

At Great Southern Grammar, cyber safety is a MASSIVE priority. This is why we care.


Student Portal - Breif Detail

Great Southern Grammar wants to make sure that when using the web, it is safe to be used.

GSG students laptops have cyber safety managers built in. These cannot be removed by students and are overrided by IT.

Student Laptops

Students are given the trust when using their computers.

CISCO Umbrella

Computers are easily managed by CISCO Umbrella.

Umbrella blocks any website that isnt safe or right for the student to visit.

Students can understand why they cannot visit that site because it will show the attempt access website and the reason why of access denial.

Ad Block

Ad's are annyoing, specially in the middle of the class. This is why we have enabled Ad Block.

Ad Block is on every browser that the student is given and cannot be taken off.

Ad Block is for free and can be accessed on the Chrome Extention Store (If you are using Edge, you can still download it through the Chrome store.
